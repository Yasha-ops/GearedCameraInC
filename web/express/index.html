G.C.C - EPITA

What is GCC ?



The principle



GCC is a 3D printed device that is compatible with every time of camera/webcam. This techonological device's goal is to make the attached camera follow you in every of your movements.



How does it work?



The idea is simple: we apply various algorithms in order to detect the center of your head. Once we have the coordonates, we send a signal to the hardware that activates different gears using step by step motors in order to make the camera move on one of the 2 axis.

The utility



From the online teachers to the classical manager, this device could help a large variety of people. It will follow the speaker in his speech, without him ever having to care about being in the camera field.



The pros



Simple and easy to use


Light and portable


Fast and accurate
