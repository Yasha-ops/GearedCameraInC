What is GCC ?



The principle



GCC is a 3D printed device that is compatible with every time of camera/webcam. This techonological device's goal is to make the attached camera follow you in every of your movements.

How does it work?



The idea is simple: we apply various algorithms in order to detect the center of your head. Once we have the coordonates, we send a signal to the hardware that activates different gears using step by step motors in order to make the camera move on one of the 2 axis.

But who would use such a device?



From the online teachers to the classical manager, this device could help a large variety of people. It will follow the speaker in his speech, without him ever having to care about being in the camera field.

PROS 2


Adaptative

  1. The GCC support can handle various types of cameras as well as specific dimensions including the biggest ones.
  2. The "U shaped" support allows to fix the camera correctly in order to prevent any sliding.

Simple and easy to use

  1. As everything is programmed by our smart ingeneers, the GCC support is totally automatic and do not need any user interaction!
  2. Just plug the usb port into you computer and you are ready to go!

Light and portable

  1. The GCC support is made of ABS plastic and printed in 3D. This material allows to have a force resistive component while being fairly light.
  2. The weight of the supoort being small, u can without difficulties bring it to your office or your home everyday if you feel like it!
