Modes

Learn how to configure settings for Scribbler, such as your syntax highlighting preference and the default saving folder location.

Nbr Name Descritption
0 Simple Basic Webcam. Check to see if your hair is good or not ;)
1 Skin Detection (Noisy) See how our algorithim detects your Human-Parts.
2 Skin Detection (Refined) Human parts highlighted and cleaned.
3 Robert's Edge Detection image (Raw) See how we outline your facial features.
4 Robert's Edge Detection image (Refined) All of your features are highlighted.
5 Circle Detection See how we are able to find where (Most Likely) your face is!
6 Follow me The camera will follow you. The final desired product.

Mode 6 is the mode that allows the camera to follow you. The other modes are just so that you can marvel at our product! For Maximum results, please try and stand in the middle of the frame, and allow for calibration. Afterwards, proceed with your movements.
