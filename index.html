G.C.C - EPITA

What is GCC ?




The principle



GCC is a 3D printed device that is compatible with every type of camera/webcam. This techonological device's goal is to make the attached camera follow your every movements.



How does it work?



The idea is simple: we apply various algorithms in order to detect the center of your head. Once we have the coordonates, we send a signal to the hardware that activates different gears using step by step motors in order to make the camera move on one of the 2 axis.

The utility



From the online teachers to the classical manager, this device could help a large variety of people. It will follow the speaker as they present their speech, without them ever having to care about being in the camera's field.
